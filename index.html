The public site explains Proofworks. The app is where submissions, reviews, and Degrees happen.
What happens after I click a path?
You’ll sign in, create a claim, and attach proof. Stewards review in good faith. If it meets the standard, a Degree of Proof is issued with a serial and an audit trail.
Why this exists
Resumes are narratives. Credentials are often gatekeeping. Proofworks is a signal: verifiable work, reviewed by humans, stored as a permanent record.
What you’re paying for
  • Facilitation of your submission and proof storage.
  • Delegation to independent reviewers (stewards).
  • Audit trail for decisions and issuance.
What you’re not buying
  • You do not buy a Degree of Proof.
  • You do not buy a database entry.
  • Degrees are earned, and may be revoked or changed without notice.
