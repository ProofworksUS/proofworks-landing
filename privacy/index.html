Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-03-02
What we collect
  • Account identifiers (email, UID) and basic profile fields.
  • Submissions you create, including claim text, metadata, and uploaded proof.
  • Review decisions and audit traces connected to submissions.
  • Payment status metadata required to operate the service.
How we use data
  • To run the workflow (drafts, submissions, reviews, issuance).
  • To prevent fraud and protect system integrity.
  • To provide support and respond to inquiries.
Public vs private Degrees
Some Degrees may be marked public, which means they can be searched and viewed. Private Degrees are intended to be accessible only through authenticated access or direct links.
Data retention
Proofworks may retain submissions, proof, decisions, and audit traces to protect the integrity of the registry. Unpaid drafts may expire and be purged from active workflows.
Contact
Privacy questions: admin@proofworks.us (or use info@proofworks.us for general questions).
